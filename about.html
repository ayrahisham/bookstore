ABOUT LIGHTBULB TROLLEY BOOKSHOP SINGAPORE

CORPORATE INFORMATION

LightBulb Trolley Bookshop Singapore was founded by Michi Tan on 22nd January 1927. Our venture began with the opening of LightBulb Trolley Bookhop in Downtown East Store in 2012.

To-date, we have 13 bookstores in Singapore, including 18 others around Singapore. We also have 6 corporate sales offices spread throughout Singapore, as well as 3 export/liaison offices.

Ever entrepreneurial and embodying the spirit of kaizen, LightBulb Trolley Bookhop is committed to provide excellent services and create a pleasant ambience amongst our bookstores, so as to satisfy and delight all our customers.
